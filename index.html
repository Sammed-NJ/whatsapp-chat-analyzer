How to Export WhatsApp Chat

  1. 1 Open WhatsApp and go to your group chat
  2. 2 Tap on the group name at the top
  3. 3 Scroll down and tap "Export Chat"
  4. 4 Choose "Without Media" for faster processing
  5. 5 Save the .txt file and upload it here
